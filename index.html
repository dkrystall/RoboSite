Cal State LA RoboSub

 
 
 
What is a RoboSub?

 

A RoboSub is a completely autonomous robotic submarine. Our vehicles are designed to independently navigate underwater obstacle courses using different sensors and algorithms.

The Competition

 

RoboSub teams from all over the world travel to San Diego every year at the end of July to compete in a week-long competition that tests the limits of each team's submarine.

Team Organization

 

Our team consists of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science students, each working on different parts of the project. 

How do I Join?

 

Our team has a short application for new members. See the "Contact" tab to be added to our email list or to submit your application online!
